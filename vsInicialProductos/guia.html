Frequently Asked Questions

Is Glasp FREE to use?

Yes, Glasp is free for everyone. You can unlimitedly highlight text on websites and PDF documents, organize your highlights and notes, and even export these to various note-taking appsall at no cost.

For users seeking more privacy and enhanced features, Glasp Pro is available. By upgrading to Glasp Pro, you gain access to unlimited private highlights and notes, allowing you to keep your insights just to yourself or share them selectively.

Is there a mobile app or tablet app for Glasp?

Yes, Glasp is available on mobile devices. We offer dedicated apps for both iOS and Android platforms, ensuring you can easily access and use Glasp on your smartphones and tablets. Additionally, Glasp can be accessed through any mobile browser for convenience. For more details and download options, please visit our Glasp Mobile Apps page.

Do my highlights and notes sync across devices?

Yes, all your highlights and notes seamlessly sync across devices when you use your Glasp account. Our automatic sync feature ensures that your highlights and notes are always updated and accessible on any device, keeping your learning synchronized wherever you go!

Can I share my highlights and notes with others?

Absolutely! You can share your highlights and notes with friends, peers, and followers on various social media platforms. Additionally, you can embed your highlights and notes on your blog or website for wider accessibility. When you share a link to your highlights and notes, anyone can view them without needing to sign up for Glasp, making it easy to spread knowledge and insights.

How can I contact Glasp support?

You can contact Glasp support by sending an email to hi [at] glasp.co. Our support team will get back to you as soon as possible. You can also reach out to us on Twitter or LinkedIn

Why are you building Glasp?

Mission: Glasp stands for "Greatest Legacy Accumulated as Shared Proof," embodying our commitment to democratize access to individual learning and experiences. We aim to capture and visualize the contributions of individuals to the collective knowledge of humanity, serving as a utilitarian legacy that enriches and educates future generations.

Vision: Our vision is to simplify access to a world of highlighted information. We strive to make it possible to access the essence of human knowledge and insights with just one click, enhancing the efficiency and reach of learning worldwide.

What drives us to build Glasp?

At Glasp, we are passionate about enhancing how people research and learn. By facilitating the highlighting and organization of key quotes and ideas from across the web, we enable individuals to learn more effectively and efficiently. Sharing these insights fosters connections among like-minded individuals, creating a community of learners who inspire and educate one another.

Additionally, Glasp offers the innovative feature of building your own AI clone using your collection of highlights and notes. This AI clone not only generates personalized insights and recommendations based on your learning history but also acts as a digital legacy, preserving your knowledge and wisdom for future generations. This feature exemplifies our dedication to using cutting-edge technology to enhance and preserve human knowledge.
